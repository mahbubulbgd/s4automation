About S4 Automation

Leading with expertise, reliability, and innovation in marine automation, power, electrical, and electronic systems.

Who We Are

S4Automation Private Limited is a specialized marine automation servicing company based in Singapore, founded by experienced marine automation engineers with decades of hands-on expertise. We are dedicated to delivering comprehensive solutions in marine automation, control, and power systems.

Our team combines deep technical knowledge with practical maritime experience, ensuring that every solution we deliver is not only technically sound but also operationally effective in the demanding marine environment.

Our Expertise

With many years of proven experience in the marine automation sector, we understand the critical nature of reliable, efficient, and cutting-edge automation systems aboard vessels. Our team specializes in:

Marine Automation Systems

Complete automation solutions for vessels of all types and sizes

Control Systems Engineering

Advanced control system design, installation, and optimization

Power Management Systems

Efficient power distribution and energy management solutions

System Integration

Seamless integration of automation components and subsystems

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled servicing to ensure optimal system performance

Emergency Repairs

Rapid response troubleshooting and repair services
