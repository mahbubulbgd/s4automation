About

We are a leading provider of marine automation solutions, dedicated to improving the safety, efficiency, and performance of maritime operations. Specializing in the servicing, installation, and optimization of marine engine control systems, we ensure vessels operate at peak reliability.

Our expertise extends to marine navigation and communication electronics, offering full-spectrum support from hardware and software to complex network systems. As marine automation engineers, we diagnose and resolve critical control system issues with precision and speed.

Driven by innovation and a commitment to excellence, we deliver dependable engineering solutions and responsive service. Our goal is to be your trusted partner, keeping your fleet running safely, smoothly, and efficiently through expert technical support and cutting-edge automation technology.
